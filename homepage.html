The Teams


The Blue Bears are the tactical giants and the reigning champions of the intramurals, relying on their rock-solid defense and calm composure to outlast their opponents.


The Red Bulldogs bring a gritty, high-energy hustle to the field, known for their relentless physical play and "never say die" attitude.


The Yellow Tigers are the charismatic playmakers of the group, dominating the game with natural flair, agility, and fierce competitive strikes.


The Green Hornets prioritize speed and precision, using lightning-fast transitions to sting their rivals before they can react.
